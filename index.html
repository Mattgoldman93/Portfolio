About

I've always been one to approach tasks differently and view challenges from varying perspectives as I solve them. My love of learning and examination led me to study philosophy as an undergraduate. It is surprising to many people that from this background I would enter the tech industry; at their cores, however, philosophy and programming are both works of logic and problem solving. My aim as a developer is to build technology that brings new possibilities and opportunities for personal edification to as many people as possible. As a lifelong learner I fully intend to continue developing my skills constantly in order to reach this aim.
